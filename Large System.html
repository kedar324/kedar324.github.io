Large System

Bill of Material

Solar Panel

₹ 6000 - 7500

Two 100 Wp - 150 Wp Solar panels with Voc of 18 - 19V can be used for this system.

Lead Acid Battery

₹ 2000 - 3000

One Lead acid battery of  10 - 12 Ah can be used for this sytem. These could be batteries used for automotive applications like 2 wheelers, cars, tractors or UPS replacement battery.

12V Car Inverter

₹ 1800 - 2500

These are inverters used to draw power from automotive power socket. They are rated from 20W upto 200W output. For charging a laptop and a mobile phone simultaneously, minimum 100W ouput is reccomemded.

Spade Connectors

₹ 20

1 sets of spade connectors will be required. Both the Solar PV connections and the car inverter cables will be connected to one connector. 

MC 4 & Y-Connectors   

₹ 600

These connectors are required for power take-off from Solar PV Panels.

Cables

₹ 200

Appropriate length of cables would be required to take the power output from Solar panels placed outside to the battery indoors. 6 sq mm cable is recommended for lower power loss
